## Mission

Develop an institutional-grade automated trading platform emphasizing:

- capital preservation
- robust risk management
- low-latency execution
- modular architecture
- explainable trading decisions
- production reliability

---

## Long-Term Goals

- Multi-strategy trading engine
- Portfolio-level risk management
- Institutional execution quality
- AI-assisted research workflow
- Continuous strategy validation
- Automated deployment pipeline

---

## Non-Negotiable Principles

1. Never risk capital to simplify code.
2. Every strategy must be backtested.
3. Every production change must be reviewable.
4. Every bug must have a root-cause analysis.
5. Every architectural decision must be documented.
6. Risk management overrides profitability.
7. No hidden business logic.
8. No duplicated execution logic.
9. Every API interaction must handle failure.
10. Every deployment must be reversible.
